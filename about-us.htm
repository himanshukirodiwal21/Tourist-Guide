About Us

Welcome to KOTA-TOUR, your gateway to an enchanting journey through the heart of Rajasthan, India. We are a passionate team of locals with a deep-rooted love for our city, Kota. Our mission is to share the rich cultural heritage, breathtaking landscapes, and warm hospitality of this region with travelers from around the world.

Our Mission

KOTA-TOUR was born out of a desire to showcase the hidden gems of Kota and its surrounding areas to foreign visitors. Kota, often referred to as the "Education Hub of India," is a city steeped in history, art, and natural beauty. It's our firm belief that every traveler deserves to experience the magic of this incredible place.

What We Offer

At KOTA-TOUR, we specialize in crafting unforgettable, immersive experiences for foreigners visiting Kota. Our tours are carefully designed to provide a perfect blend of culture, history, and adventure. Whether you're an explorer seeking ancient palaces and temples, a nature enthusiast yearning for serene landscapes, or an art lover in search of local craftsmanship, we have the ideal tour package for you.
